# AirBnb-Dashboard
Project Title:- 

                Global Airbnb Performance Analysis Dashboard – Power BI


Short Description:- 
                      
                    An interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze Airbnb listings across major global cities. The dashboard provides insights into market share,                        pricing trends, host performance, and customer ratings to help understand how Airbnb listings perform across locations and property types.


Tech Stack:- 

            1)Power BI – Data visualization & dashboard development
            2)Power Query – Data cleaning and transformation
            3)DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) – Calculated measures and KPIs
            4)Excel / CSV datasets – Data storage and preprocessing


Data Source:- 

              1. Dataset sourced from Maven Analytics Airbnb Dataset
              2. Contains Airbnb listing information such as:
               *Listings
               *Cities
               *Hosts
               *Property types
               *Reviews
               *Ratings
               *Price categories


Features / Highlights:- 

                        1. Business Problem
                        Airbnb operates across multiple cities with thousands of listings, making it difficult to quickly understand:
                          *Which cities dominate the Airbnb market
                          *How different property types perform
                          *Which locations provide the best guest experience
                          *How listing growth has evolved over time
                        Without a centralized dashboard, identifying trends and making data-driven decisions becomes challenging.
                        
                        2. Goal of the Dashboard
                        The goal of this dashboard is to:
                          *Analyze Airbnb listing distribution across major cities
                          *Evaluate pricing differences between property types
                          *Compare guest ratings across locations
                          *Track growth trends of listings over time
                          *Provide insights to help stakeholders understand market performance and customer satisfaction

                      
Key Visuals / Charts Used:-

                            1. KPI Cards
                            The dashboard displays key metrics including:
                            *Total Listings → 279,712
                            *Cities Covered → 10
                            *Hosts → 182,024
                            *Property Types → 144
                            *Total Reviews → 5,373,143
                            These KPIs provide a quick snapshot of Airbnb's global scale.

                            2. Market Share by City (Pareto Chart)
                            A stacked bar chart with cumulative percentage line showing:
                            *Superhost vs Non-Superhost listings
                            *City-wise distribution of listings
                            *Cumulative contribution to total listings
                            Insight:
                            Paris, New York City, and Sydney together contribute nearly half of the total listings and reviews.

                            3. Average Price by Property Type
                            A horizontal bar chart comparing prices for:
                            *Hotel rooms
                            *Entire places
                            *Shared rooms
                            *Private rooms
                            Insight:
                            Hotel rooms are the most expensive option, while private rooms offer the most affordable stays.

                            4. Ratings Analysis (Heatmap)
                            A heatmap table visualization displaying ratings across multiple categories:
                            *Accuracy
                            *Cleanliness
                            *Communication
                            *Location
                            *Value for Money
                            Insight:
                            *Mexico City and Rio de Janeiro show the highest overall ratings.
                            *Hong Kong and Istanbul have comparatively lower ratings.

                            5. Average Rating Comparison
                            *A column chart ranking cities based on overall rating scores.
                            *This allows quick identification of top-performing and low-performing markets.

                            6. Listing Growth Over Time
                            A multi-line trend chart showing growth of:
                            *Total listings
                            *Entire place listings
                            *Private room listings
                            *Shared rooms
                            *Hotel rooms
                            From 2008 to 2020.
                            Insight:
                            *Airbnb experienced rapid growth until 2015 (peak listings).
                            *Listing growth slowed due to regulatory changes.
                            *A sharp decline occurred during the COVID-19 Pandemic.


Business Impact / Insights:-

                            Key insights derived from the dashboard:
                            1)Top Cities Dominate Market Share
                            Paris, New York, and Sydney account for a large portion of listings and reviews.
                            2)Entire Homes Drive Revenue
                            Entire property listings command higher prices compared to shared or private rooms.
                            3)Customer Satisfaction Varies by City
                            Some cities consistently deliver better guest experiences.
                            4)Cleanliness and Value for Money Are Weak Points
                            These two rating categories often score lower compared to communication and location.
                            5)Market Growth Plateau
                            After strong expansion, Airbnb faced regulatory and pandemic-related challenges.
